## Context

Creates the composable operation seam — named invoke, explicit time budget, structured trace — that turns any boundary call into readable pass/fail evidence, repo-local with no fleet dependency.

_Advances: P2, P7_

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A test suite invokes stub operations through the runner: a passing stub yields a trace record with operation name, duration, and outcome; a stub that never resolves is cut off at its time budget with an explicit timeout failure — and the suite itself completes in seconds, never hanging.
