# Demo Runway — Vision

Demo Runway makes the first hours of a hackathon compound instead of disappear into
scaffolding. It starts as a working, public, Cloudflare-first web app and gives a small
team a proven path from sponsor docs and a product idea to a convincing core demo.

The project standardizes the runway, not the demo. It pre-solves deployment, secrets,
responsive presentation, collaboration, testing, tracing, and failure handling while
leaving product behavior and provider choices to the idea. The default experience is
opinionated enough to prevent framework-by-inertia, but composable enough to accept the
model API, storage system, database, CMS, authentication, and billing the day requires.

Success has two horizons. On Day 1, the demo is live, observable, hard to leave hanging,
and easy for teammates and stakeholders to shape. On Day 2, it can accept real users,
earn revenue, persist as a low-cost portfolio project, or be handed off without depending
on infrastructure maintained by the template author.
